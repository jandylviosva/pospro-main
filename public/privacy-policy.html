Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

POS Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is a cloud-synced Point of Sale software service operated by POS Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our software, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using POS Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Service.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Store Information

  • Store name, owner name, and email address provided during activation
  • Login credentials (passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text)
  • Device names and device IDs for registered POS devices
  • License codes and activation records

Business Data

  • Product catalog (names, prices, categories, stock levels, images)
  • Sales orders, transaction history, and payment records
  • Shift reports, cashier accounts, and expense logs
  • Inventory adjustments and purchase orders

Technical Data

  • Device last-seen timestamps for sync tracking
  • Sync activity logs (what was changed and when)
  • Browser/device type for compatibility purposes
We do not collect payment card numbers, government IDs, or any sensitive personal information from your customers. POS Pro is a business management tool — your customers' personal data is not stored in our system.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the POS Pro service
  • To sync your store data across registered devices via Supabase cloud storage
  • To send one-time login codes (OTP) to your registered email via Resend
  • To send shift reports and inventory exports to your email when requested
  • To manage your license, device slots, and subscription plan
  • To provide developer/technical support when you open a support session
  • To improve and troubleshoot the Service

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties. We do not use your business data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

POS Pro uses the following third-party services to operate:

Supabase

Your store data (products, orders, accounts, settings) is stored in Supabase, a PostgreSQL-based cloud database. Data is stored on servers in the Asia-Pacific region. Supabase complies with SOC 2 Type 2 and GDPR standards.

Resend

We use Resend to send transactional emails (OTP login codes, shift reports). Your email address is shared with Resend only for the purpose of delivering these emails.

Vercel

Our web application and API functions are hosted on Vercel. Vercel processes request data (IP address, headers) as part of serving the application.

All data transmitted between your devices and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data Retention

Your store data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your store data from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as license audit records).

Backup copies may persist for up to 7 days after deletion due to Supabase's backup retention policy.

6. Data Security

  • All passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage — we cannot recover your plain-text password
  • Owner and developer login uses one-time codes (OTP) sent to your registered email
  • API access uses signed session tokens with expiry times
  • Developer support sessions are time-limited (auto-expire after 30 minutes) and require explicit activation
  • All database write operations for sensitive actions are performed server-side using a service role key, never exposed to the browser

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique email address for your POS Pro account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about your store
  • Correction — update incorrect store or account information via the Owner Portal
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export — export your product catalog and order history as CSV from the POS app at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@pospro-portal.com.

8. Cookies

POS Pro does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Our web application uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage to maintain your login session on your device. These are not shared with third parties and are cleared when you sign out.

9. Children's Privacy

POS Pro is a business software service intended for use by adults operating retail or food service businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify active store owners via email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
